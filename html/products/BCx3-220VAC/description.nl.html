Expert Q&A

Werkt dit met 36V batterijen? +
Yes! The DATE BCx3 fully supports 36V systems. Simply select the 36V mode, and it will charge your battery safely to a maximum of 42.0V. Perfect for smaller scooters and standard e-bikes. Original MATE CITY are also using 36V batteries. If not sure, check the label of your battery!
Wat zijn de instellingen voor 48V-batterijen? +
For 48V systems, you have two powerful options:
  • 90% Mode: Charges to 53V. Use this for daily riding to double your battery's lifespan.
  • 100% Mode: Charges to 54.6V. Use this for maximum range on long trips.
Kan ik het gebruiken met mijn 52V batterij? +
Absolutely. The DATE BCx3 is natively designed for 52V performance:
  • 90% Mode: Charges to 57V to protect cell chemistry and extend cycle life.
  • 100% Mode: Charges to 58.8V for peak power and range.
Beschermt het mij tegen verbindingsvonken? +
Categorically YES. Connect the adapter to the battery first, then the yellow XT90S Anti-Spark plugs. The integrated resistor eliminates the 'pop' completely.
Waarom investeren in een professionele lader? +
Think of it as insurance for your battery. While generic chargers cut corners, causing heat and stress, a professional charger like the DATE BCx3 uses:
  • Smart Thermal Management: Keeps cells cool.
  • Precision Cut-off: Prevents overcharging.
  • Variable Speed: Gives you control.
